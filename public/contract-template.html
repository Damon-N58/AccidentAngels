Scholar Transport Agreement

Issued by: Accident Angels (Pty) Ltd in association with GETS

1. Introduction

This agreement is entered into between the Service Provider (the driver) and the Parent/Guardian (the parent), as identified in the contract details, for the purpose of providing safe and reliable scholar transport for the named learner between the stated pickup and dropoff addresses.

This agreement is concluded electronically in accordance with the Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002 (ECTA) and constitutes a valid and binding agreement.

2. Service Obligations

  1. The Service Provider agrees to transport the Learner safely on all school days.
  2. The Service Provider will collect the Learner from the Pickup Address and deliver them to the Dropoff Address as specified.
  3. The Service Provider holds a valid PDP (Professional Driving Permit), police clearance certificate, and passenger liability insurance, all current and approved by GETS.
  4. The Service Provider will not exceed the vehicle's licensed passenger capacity at any time.
  5. The Service Provider will notify the Parent at least 24 hours in advance if the service will be unavailable on any school day.

3. Payment Terms

  1. The Parent agrees to pay the Monthly Fee as specified in the contract details by the 1st of each month.
  2. Failure to pay within 7 days of the due date may result in suspension of the transport service.
  3. All payment disputes must be raised within 30 days of the charge.

4. Termination

  1. Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice.
  2. The Accident Angels platform may be used to communicate notice of termination.
  3. Any outstanding payments at time of termination remain due and payable.

5. Liability

  1. The Service Provider carries valid passenger liability insurance for the duration of this agreement.
  2. Neither Accident Angels nor GETS is a party to the transport arrangement and accepts no liability for incidents during transport.
  3. The Service Provider is solely responsible for the safe transport of the Learner.

6. Compliance

This agreement is subject to the Gauteng Department of Education scholar transport regulations as amended from time to time. The Service Provider undertakes to maintain all required compliance documentation throughout the duration of this agreement.

7. Jurisdiction

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Republic of South Africa.
